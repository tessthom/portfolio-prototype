Who is she

Design-Build,
Forever

Before a spine injury shook my world up, I used to be a woodworker and artist. I've built everything from the set of Color Purple to some of the fanciest furniture you can image. My favorite build is a tie between hand-placing every single piece of "bark" on this opera set, and being asked to paint a beloved client's entire staircase in an homage to Richard Diebenkorn.

Child of the Lowcountry

I'm from a little island off the southernmost tip of South Carolina. I grew up covered in pluff mud, throwin' a cast net from the rocking bow of a jon boat to fill coolers up with the best shrimp in the whole wide world. Whenever I'm stressed', I just find the nearest body of water get my feet wet for a while.

Gettin' Loose

While I'm drawn to painstaking precision in my work work, I love making forms based on pure, in-the-moment, hand-to-material intuition by throwing clay on a pottery wheel. I got myself some standing legs for ol' Lucinda, my beloved Brent CXC named after the legendary Lucinda Williams, so I can keep from hurting myself, too! Clay is so forgiving and infinitely recyclable til vitrified. I love the freedom of making stuff with 0 consequences like that.

Fun Fact #4
